Project name: Stock Intelligence Copilot
Problem. Retail investors make time-sensitive trading decisions using delayed news, social media noise, and fragmented data — the same decisions institutional traders make with real-time research teams and Bloomberg terminals.
Solution. Stock Intelligence Copilot uses AI to instantly synthesize earnings calls, SEC filings, volume signals, and market sentiment for any ticker into a plain-language briefing a retail investor can act on in under 30 seconds.
Target user. Self-directed retail investors aged 18–35 who actively trade on platforms like Robinhood or Fidelity but lack access to institutional-grade research — specifically the 58 million Americans who own individual stocks without a financial advisor.
Success criteria.

Briefing generated in under 10 seconds per ticker vs. 20–40 minutes of manual research
Covers all four key information types (earnings, filings, volume, sentiment) in a single view
Plain-language readability score accessible to a non-finance audience (Flesch-Kessler grade 8 or below)
Eliminates need for 4+ separate tools (Yahoo Finance, SEC EDGAR, news aggregators, social sentiment trackers)
Scalable to any publicly traded U.S. equity with no manual data input required
